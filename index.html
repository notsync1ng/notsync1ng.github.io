info

Osprey is a vanilla+ Java/Bedrock cross-compatible Minecraft server based on the latest game version. We are launching season 2 at the end of November following a successful 6 month long first season.

Our server features enhanced terrain and new vanilla-like world structures, as well as some additional enchantments, animations and quality-of-life fixes. We also have Discord chat integration and in-game voice chat support.

We also have a silly lore, make events around it and post event recaps on our YouTube channel!
